NORTHERN NOMADS v THE BORDERERS.
These teams met on Mr Bewick's ground at Close
House yesterday. Batting first, the Nomads ran up a
total of 228, chiefly by the fine play of P Ashworth (65)
and H Jenner Fust (57), both of whom scored freely off
the bowling, their partnership carrying the score from 114
to 219.
On the Borderers going in wickets fell in rapid
succession until Fogg-Elliot and O Liddell became associ-
ated, when nine men were out for 67. By steady play
they increased the total to 102, when the heavy rainfall
caused the game to be abandoned, thus robbing the
Nomads of a certain victory. Score:—
