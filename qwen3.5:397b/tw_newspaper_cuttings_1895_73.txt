EXETER v. NORTH DEVON.
This match was played at Graslawn yesterday in beautiful weather and before a good number of spectators. Exeter went to the wickets first, and were not dismissed until they had put together the good total of 276 runs. The chief scorers were J. N. Nicholls 140, whose total included five 6's and nine 4's, W. Ashford 51, and W. B. Carter 28. North Devon were all dismissed for 149. Exeter thus winning by 129 runs. The best scorers for the visitors were F. Townsend 58 (not out), E. H. Topham 18, and S. F. Middleton 14. Below are the scores:—

EXMOUTH v. SOUTH HAMPSTEAD.
This two days match was commenced at Exmouth on Wednesday. Play was continued yesterday in brilliant weather, and proved a most interesting game. In their first innings South Hampstead only totalled 73, but they did much better in their second, running up a score of 308, the chief contributors being Hassell (69), E. Robinson (not out 71) and Brown (39). Exmouth made 152 and 103, their top scorer being J. H. Copleston (85-39).

YORKSHIRE v. ESSEX.
In this match, at Harrogate yesterday, Essex scored 87 in their first innings, and 39 for three wickets in their second. Yorkshire's first innings produced 117.

TORQUAY CRICKET MONTH.
INCOGNITI v. ISHMAELITES.
This two days' match was concluded at the Torquay Cricket ground yesterday, the result being an even draw. For the Ishmaelites, A. E. Heatley made a fine 83, which included a 6 and seven 4's, and for their opponents, A. C. S. Glover scored 60, and this also included a 6. Scores:—Incogniti, 213 and 18 for two wickets; Ishmaelites, 100 and 262 for 8 wickets, innings declared closed.

TEIGNBRIDGE v. NORTH DEVON. — Played yesterday at Teignbridge, and resulted in a draw. Teignbridge, for the loss of three wickets, made 232, the total score of the North Devon team being 80, with Mr A Clarke not out and Mr W W Arthur to bat. For Teignbridge Mr E B Sandilands made 68, Mr F P Hutchinson 102, and Mr B Johnson 53. Scores :—Teignbridge, 243 ; North Devon, 83.
