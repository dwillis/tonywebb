STANTONBURY, ST. JAMES' v. STOKE STRATFORD MUTUAL.—This match was played at Stantonbury on Friday, August 2nd, and resulted in a win for the visitors by 9 runs. This was brought about chiefly by Allen's 15 runs in the 1st innings, and the bowling of Allen, E. Martin and Peddar the latter performing the hat trick. B own and Cockcroft for the Mutual bowled well.
