KING'S LYNN v. DISS.-This Junior Cup match was
brought off at Lynn on Saturday last. It must be
said that the Diss men were greatly disappointed after
making the long journey to find such a bad ground and
wicket to play on. All was in favour of the bowlers,
as in consequence of a dip towards each wicket the
ball shot in very quickly. Worman and Ruscoe only
reached double figures. The feature of the innings
was a catch in the long field by Gausson. Diss com-
menced with Hipkin and A. G. Bobby, both of whom
played carefully. When the score stood at 17, Hipkin
was unfortunately run out, and Cole shared the same
fate. At lunch time the score was 29 for three wickets.
The match terminated in a victory for Diss by 13 runs.
Leggett four for 28, Ruscoe three for 15, Ireland six
for 26, and Hipkin four for 18 were the bowlers. King's
Lynn-A Robinson b Ireland 0, J Spense b Ireland 2,
J N Worman b Ireland 12, Rev A Rogers b Hipkin 5,
Rev W Boyce b Hipkin 6, J Bowen c J Bobby b
Hipkin 1, C Archdale b Ireland 0, B Ruscoe not out 15,
F Feltwell c Gausson b Ireland 0, H Winearls b
Hipkin 1, E Leggett c Williams b Ireland 0, extras 1;
total 47. Diss-A (I Bobby b Worman 10, S T Hipkin
run out 11, F G Cole run out 1, M W Ireland c
Rogers b Ruscoe 6, O H Chandler b Leggett 8, R A
Gausson c Sub b Leggett 8, F Madgett c Leggett b
