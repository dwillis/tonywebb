GLOUCESTER v ROSS.—Played at Ross on Wednesday on a good wicket. The home team were very weak, being without the services of Shaw (captain), Wintle, Woodward, and E. J. Smith. Mr Folle, one of the Ross players, met with an accident while fielding and sprained his left leg. Phillips played good cricket for 72, not out. Amongst his hits being three six's which brought loud applause from the spectators. Gloucester won by 81 runs. Score:—
