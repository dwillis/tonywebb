HEREFORD TIMES: SATURDAY 6 JULY 1895-2.

HEREFORDSHIRE C. AND G. v. CATHEDRAL SCHOOL.—
Played at Widemarsh on Thursday. Score:—Cathedral
School, 141 (Shepherd 55); Herefordshire C. and G., 111 for
four wickets (Phillips, not out, 71).

MALVERN COLLEGE v KIBBORNE SCHOOL.—This two
days' match at Malvern ended in a win for the College by
an innings and 27 runs.

LUDLOW 2ND XI. v LUDLOW GRAMMAR SCHOOL.—Played
on Wednesday. The School won on the first innings.
Scores:— Ludlow 2nd XI., 54 and 37; School, 81.

USK 2ND XI v NEWPORT 3RD XI.—This match, which
was played on Saturday, had to be abandoned in conse-
quence of the rain after Usk, who went to the wickets first,
had made 30 runs for four wickets.

LEOMINSTER v. HEREFORD CATHEDRAL SCHOOL.—Played
at Leominster on Wednesday. Score:—Cathedral School,
51 and 128 for two wickets (Shepherd 50, and C. H. Dutton,
not out, 50); Leominster, 128 (H. C. B. Phillips 68).
