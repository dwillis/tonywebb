CAMBS. CUP COMPETITION.
On Saturday the second round of the Cambs. Cup Competition was played on Parker's Piece, between the Old Higher Grade and Sawston. The match ended in an easy win for the Old Higher Grade by 190 runs. For Sawston, Bailey was the only scorer, with five runs to his credit, extras being credited with six. MARRIED v. SINGLE.
On Tuesday a second match was played upon the same ground between Eleven Married and Eleven Single, Mr Hoare himself taking the bat with the Married Eleven. It was a well contested game, the Single team being the victors by 2 runs only. F Baker was successful with both ball and bat. Refreshments were dispensed to players and spectators, and in the evening a substantial supper was provided for all that had taken part in the game. About 9.30 down, and Mr Hoare joined the company. The tables being cleared, Mr Blake, in appropriate words, proposed the health of the host, which was drunk with musical honours. A social and harmonious evening was spent, such as Sutton cricketers have not often the opportunity of spending. Score :-
