WILTSHIRE TIMES: SATURDAY 15 JUNE 1895-2.

LEIGHTON v. NEWTON BLOWS.—Played at Newton, on Wednesday, the visitors winning. F. W. Stancomb played a fine innings of 72, and afterwards dismissed five batsmen at the wicket. Mapleton hit pluckily for the Blues. Scores:—

HEYTESBURY v. ST. BONIFACE COLLEGE.—On Saturday St. Boniface College played Heytesbury at Warminster, the game ending in a victory for the home team. Scores: Heytesbury, 54 and 27; St. Boniface College, 46 and 16 for one wicket.

WINGFIELD v. WEST ASHTON.—Played at Wingfield on Saturday: Wingfield, 65 and 42 for six wickets; West Ashton, 50.
