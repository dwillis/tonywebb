HEREFORD TIMES: SATURDAY 20 JULY 1895-3.

BROMYARD v LEDBURY.—Played at Ledbury on Saturday, and resulted in a easy victory for the visitors, Frost, the Bromyard professional performing the "hat" trick.
MONMOUTH v ROSS.—This return match was played at Monmouth on Saturday, when the visitors won by 234 runs and four wickets. For the winners Woodward played finely for his 128, as also did Lieut. Vaughan for 76.
DOWNTON CASTLE v ASHFORD COURT.—Played at Ashford on Saturday, and ended in a win for Ashford Court on the first innings. Ashford played nine men. Score: Ashford Court, 56 (P. Northover, 19; T. Matthews, not out 12); Downton Castle, 41 and 87 for five wickets (J. Mitchell, 10 and not out 13; J. Bell, 7; T. Hotchkiss, 7; and W. Hawkins, 12).
