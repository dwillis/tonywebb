The Wycombe Y.M.C.A. C.C. play Newland Bible Class on the Hys to-morrow (Saturday). Wickets pitched at 2.30.
Wycombe First XI. have no fixture for to-morrow, but the Reserves go to Marlow to play Borlase School (return). On Thursday—not Wednesday as at first arranged—the Grammar School Past and Present play the Wycombe Club, on which occasion we hear that Mr. and Mrs. Peachell have kindly offered to provide luncheon and tea for the teams. This match ought to prove a good even game, as several of the Club's First XI. are old boys and will play for the School. We also hear that on Saturday week, when the final in the Bastick Cup Competition takes place, the Vicar and Mrs. Shaw intend inviting all the members of the Club to partake of tea. Mrs. Shaw has consented to present the Cup and medals at the conclusion of the game, but we shall be able next week to give fuller details of the arrangements for the day.
St. John's C.C. play the West End United on the Recreation Ground to-morrow at 2.30.—St. John's C.C. want matches for the Saturdays in August.—R. Ford, West End-road, Wycombe.
Church Room C.C. play Wheeler End Blue Star, at Wheeler End, to-morrow, at 2.30.
