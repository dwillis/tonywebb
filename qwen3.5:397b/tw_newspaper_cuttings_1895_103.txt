CHELTENHAM v HAILEYBURY.
Frequent heavy showers caused stoppages in this match at Lord's on Saturday, and at six o'clock the game was abandoned altogether. The draw, if anything, was slightly in favour of Haileybury, who on the first innings had an advantage of 50. On both sides cricketers of more than ordinary promise took part, whilst Woof, the old Gloucestershire bowler and coach at Cheltenham, speaks very highly of one or two young cricketers that have yet to get into the eleven. Champain is to be accorded a place in the Gloucestershire team against Middlesex, whilst it is quite possible that Jarrett will be tried for Hampshire. Score :-
