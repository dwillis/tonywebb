DUNSTABLE SECOND ELEVEN v. MARKYATE STREET.
—This return match was played on the Chequers Park cricket ground, by kind permission of Mr. B. Bennett, on Saturday. Dunstable had choice of innings and batted first, Anderson and Elliott going to the wickets. Four runs only had been scored when play was interrupted by a heavy shower. After a while the game was re-commenced, and a good stand was made by the batsmen, the wickets being kept intact until 66 were scored, when Elliott was dismissed, and Anderson followed three runs later. Giddings and Beasley were settling down for run getting, when play was again interrupted for five minutes by a shower, at the end of which the villagers refused to again take the field, urging as an excuse that there was no likelihood of dismissing the Dunstable team that day. Not even the promise to declare with 10 more runs added was sufficient to induce them to take the field, and the game was therefore left in the following position :—Dunstable—W F Elliott, c and b Lee 27; O G Anderson, b Lee 34; T Giddings, not out 5; J Beasley, not out 3; extras 7; (2 wkts) 76.
