WORCESTER DAILY TIMES: MONDAY, TUESDAY 12, 13 AUGUST 1895-2.
ST. JOHN'S 2ND XI. v. COLWALL.—Played at Colwall and resulted in an easy victory for St. John's. Messrs. Beaumont, Dodson, Park, and Souch all batted well for their runs, Mr. Beaumont being top scorer with a well played innings of 45. Mr. Evans was the only double-figure scorer for Colwall. Mr. Robinson bowled splendidly for St. John's, his analysis reading 6 overs 4 maidens 6 runs and 6 wickets. Mr. Beaumont also bowled well, capturing three wickets for 15 runs. Mr. Dee was the most successful bowler for Colwall. Score:—

ST. JOHN'S 2ND XI. v. BROUGHTON.—Played at St. John's on Saturday, and resulted in an easy victory for the home team. The visitors batted first, but were disposed of for 38, Messrs. Robinson and Cross both bowling well, the former taking seven wickets for 18 runs, and the latter three for 12. St. John's then scored 39 for three wickets, when rain prevented further play. Messrs. Park, Beaumont, and Combe batted well for their respective scores. Score:—
