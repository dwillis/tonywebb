BERKSHIRE CHRONICLE: SATURDAY 10 AUGUST 1895-3.

CRICKET.
MAIDEN ERLEIGH CRICKET WEEK.
THIRD MATCH v. FREE FORESTERS.—This match, played on the Maiden Erleigh ground, terminated on Friday in last week, when again there was a large and fashionable assemblage to witness the game. The result was an easy victory for the home team, who were superior to their opponents both in batting and bowling, and also in fielding. Score:—

SUTTON'S JUNIORS v. SULLHAMSTEAD JUNIORS. — This match was played at Sullhamstead, on Wednesday, and resulted in an easy victory for the former, by an innings and nine runs. Score: Sutton's Juniors, 72; Sullhamstead, 20 and 43.
MR T. W. GIRDLESTONE'S XI v GINDLESTONES (CHARTERHOUSE). This match was played at Sunningdale School on July 31 and won by 107 runs by the home side, A. Daffen performing the "hat trick." The school thus concluded a very successful season's cricket, the Boys' Eleven having only lost five matches out of nineteen, the 2nd Eleven one out of three, whilst the Masters have only lost one out of seven played.
