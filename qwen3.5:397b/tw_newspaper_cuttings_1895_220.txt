DEVIZES AND WILTSHIRE GAZETTE: THURSDAY 13 JUNE 1895-2.

Corsham v. Trowbridge.—LEAGUE MATCH.—This match was played on the ground of the latter on Saturday last and was the second league match played by the visitors, who again proved victorious by 72 runs, though six of their batsmen were under 20 years of age, it being the youngest team ever sent out to represent the Corsham 1st XI. Fudge again was extremely useful with his bat, and was well supported by Mayo and F Hulbert. Scores:—

Corsham 2nd XI. v. Avon Vale (Bath).—This match was played at Corsham on Saturday last, and resulted in a victory for the home team by two wickets and 51 runs. Floo was in good form, contributing 57 in excellent style, and was well backed up by West, Carter, and Johnson. Carter bowled well, taking 4 wickets for 13 runs. Scores:—
