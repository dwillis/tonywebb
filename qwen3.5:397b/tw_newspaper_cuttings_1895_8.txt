DUNSTABLE ADVERTISER: SATURDAY 17 AUGUST 1895-4.
LUTON VOLUNTEERS v. REST OF BATTALION.—A match was played at Chufn Camp last week between teams representing the luton Detachment and the remainder of 3rd Vol. Battalion, the Lutonians winning by 28 runs. HOUGHTON v. WESTONING.—This match was played at Houghton Green on Monday and ended in a victory for the home team. Some excellent batting was done by F.
