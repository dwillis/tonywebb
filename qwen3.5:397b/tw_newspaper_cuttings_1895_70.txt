CRICKET.
Plymouth gained an easy victory over Stonehouse
yesterday by 99 runs, scoring 131 to 22.
The return match between Torquay and H.M.S.
Britannia was decided at Torquay yesterday, and
resulted in a victory for the home team, who scored
100 to the ship's 78. A. Dmsley (38, not out) batted
well for the winners, whilst W. J. Troughton (32)
did well for the Britannia.
EXMOUTH v. MR. A KINDERSLEY'S XI.
Mr. Kindersley took a team to Exmouth on
Wednesday, but were beaten by the first two men
of Exmouth's side. The visitors scored 110, and
Copleston and Salter put on 132 for the first wicket.
At the call of time the home XI. had made 253 for
three wickets. Scores :—
