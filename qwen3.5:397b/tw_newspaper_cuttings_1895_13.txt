READING OBSERVER: SATURDAY 25 MAY 1895-4.
CRICKET.
IMPORTANT TO SECRETARIES.—As in former years, we readily grant a portion of our space for cricket match reports, but we must impress upon secretaries the importance of sending in the scores as soon as possible after matches are played. We further desire to intimate that no score can be inserted unless it is written legibly, and the innings set out in the form in which the matches are published. Sheets for drawing up reports can be obtained gratis on applying at the Observer Office. Notices of forthcoming matches will be inserted at the top of our cricket column at the rate of 1s. for each insertion. Match cards, posters and bills of all kinds are printed with the utmost despatch and on strictly reasonable terms at the Observer Office.
