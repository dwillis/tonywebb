EASTERN DAILY PRESS: WEDNESDAY 14 AUGUST 1895.

SECOND MATCH : NORFOLK V. LINCOLNSHIRE.
There are but three alterations in the home team for the match which will be commenced this morning against Lincolnshire, and these have a tendency to improve the home combination. Sewall, Heslop, and the Rev. A. C. Davies will be displaced by the Rev. A. R. Hoare, who has been scoring pretty freely of late, Fairfax Davies, and G. F. Blake. Weather permitting, the band of the 1st V.B.N.R. will play selections to-day from 2.30 to 5.30.
