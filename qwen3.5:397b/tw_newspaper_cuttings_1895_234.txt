WELLAND AND DISTRICT v. MALVERN WELLS.
Played at Welland, on August 1st, Malvern Wells went in first. They did not fare at all well against the bowling of Mansell and Parr, and they were all dismissed for 55, Berrington (18) and Harris (11) being the highest scorers. Welland made 166 for seven wickets, owing to good batting by Gunn (31), Lord (40), Mansell (51 not out), and Parr (30). Malvern Wells in their second innings made 55, Berrington (11), and Wright (16 not out). Score:-

MR. G. A. BUCKLAND'S XI. v. MR. T. C. RECHENS' XI.--Played at Henwick on Monday. Mr. Buckland's XI batted first, and started with Messrs. G. A. Buckland and F. A. Turley. With the score at 5 Turley was dismissed by a good ball from Postle. G. F. Jones-Williams then joined Buckland, and the score had reached 70 before Jones-Williams was bowled by Watts. Messrs. H. W. Buckland and H. A. Turley also batted well for the visitors. Mr. Rechens' XI commenced to the bowling of Messrs. G. A. Buckland and F. A. Turley. Six wickets were down for 41, when rain came on, and the game ended in a draw very much in favour of Mr. Buckland's XI. For Mr. Rechens T. G. Jones-Williams played a fine innings, and Mr. Watts also batted well:-
