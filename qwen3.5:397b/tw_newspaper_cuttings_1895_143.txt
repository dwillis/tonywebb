IS E. TOMLIN TO QUALIFY FOR SOMERSETSHIRE?
We hear glowing accounts from Oxford of the abilities of E. Tomlin, brother of the county professional of that name. He is bowling on the Varsity ground to such well-known players as G. J. Mordaunt, P. F. Warner, H. K. Foster, and all the best cricketers up there, and his services are evidently appreciated, as we hear that he has already been approached with a view to his playing for Somersetshire. This would prove a great loss to Leicestershire, and our county committee would do well to take the matter in hand.
