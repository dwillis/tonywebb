SOUTH WALES DAILY STAR: MONDAY 19 AUGUST 1895-2.

BASSALEG v. NEWPORT BANKS.
This annual match, played at Bassaleg on Thursday, resulted, as the subjoined score shows, in a narrow victory for the home team. For the home team Jones captured four wickets. Cound, for the visitors, being accredited the same number. Rodney Williams and H. G. Davies were respectively the top scorers.

CAXTONIANS v. NEWPORT NORTHERN.
This match was played on the Marshes on Saturday. This being the first match for the Caxtonians, much interest was manifested by their friends, a large number of whom put in an appearance. The feature of the game was the splendid bowling of E. T. Smith. Both eleven's were very keen in the fielding.

RANJITSINHJI IN BRIGHTON.
At the conclusion of the Sussex v. Lancashire Cricket Match on Wednesday, a member of the staff of the "Brighton Examiner" had an interesting interview with Mr. Ranjitsinhji in regard to his connection with Sussex Cricket. The interview is fully reported in the "Brighton Examiner" of Friday, August 16th.
