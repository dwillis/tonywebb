DUNSTABLE ADVERTISER: SATURDAY 31 AUGUST 1895-2.

DUNSTABLE SECOND ELEVEN v. CARTER'S C.C.—
The long list of victories won by the Town second
eleven was augmented on Saturday by another win,
when Carter's C.C. was defeated on the Town ground,
after a good game, by 45 runs. The batting of the
homesters was good, Messrs. Love, Gray, Tompkins,
Poulton and Wright played a good game. For the
visitors H. Bunker played well, as also did E. Mooring
and J. Timm. Score :—
