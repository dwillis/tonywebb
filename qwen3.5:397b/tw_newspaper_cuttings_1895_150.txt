BURTON LATIMER v. WELLINGBORO' ALL SAINTS' B.—
Played at Wellingboro' on Saturday, the visitors winning by 114 runs.
KETTERING WANDERERS v. OASTON.—Played at Kettering on Saturday, the visitors winning by 39 runs to 96.
LINCOLN.—The Bracebridge Asylum eleven met the Lincoln Imps on Saturday. The Asylum went in first, and scored 151 for eight wickets, Bennett contributing 85. The Imps then went in, but had only scored 41 when their last wicket fell. The losers would only have equalled a very poor total had it not been for a well-hit 25 by Cox.
BOTSWELL INDEPENDENT v. KETTERING BRITANNIA.—At Botswell on Saturday, an interesting game ending in a draw.
