HEREFORD TIMES: SATURDAY 27 JULY 1895-3.

"HEREFORD TIMES" CRICKET CHALLENGE TROPHY.
SEMI-FINAL.
PRESTEIGN V MALVERN.
This match was played at Presteign on Thursday in last week, the home side winning by 33 runs after a good game. For the winners the Rev. H. G. Green-Price, F. L. Evelyn, W. T. Williams, and W. Green-Price batted finely, whilst F. Davies met with most success with the ball. The feature of Malvern's display was the all round play of W. H. Hartland, who secured four of the Presteign wickets, besides carrying his bat through the innings for 24. Score:-

SHROPSHIRE V LUDLOW AND SOUTH SHROP. SHIRE.
This match was played on Saturday on the Shrewsbury ground. Ludlow went in first, and scored the good total of 201 for nine wickets, although F. L. Evelyn was absent, F. H. Stilwell (52), Clarke (30), Leake (31), Hartling (21), and Willis (10), all batted well.
