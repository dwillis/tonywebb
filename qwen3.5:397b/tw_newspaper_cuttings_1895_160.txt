GUNTON v. BLOFIELD AND DISTRICT.
Played at Gunton on Thursday, and was won by Blofield and District. The Gunton Club was not able to put a very strong team into the field, but its representatives might possibly have averted defeat but for bad fielding, several easy catches being missed.

BECCLES COLLEGE v. YARMOUTH R.A.
Played on the school ground on Thursday. The school winning the toss sent in the soldiers, and quickly dismissed them for 47. Jessop, after taking eight wickets for 14 runs, made 218 not out.
