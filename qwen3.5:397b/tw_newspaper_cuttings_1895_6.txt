DUNSTABLE BOROUGH GAZETTE: WEDNESDAY 14 AUGUST 1895 - 5.

HOUGHTON v WESTONING.
Played on Houghton Green on Monday, and
resulted in a victory for the home team. The
principal feature of the game was the excellent
form of F. Love, who carried his bat through the
innings for 46; A. Cook (57) also batted well.
