CATHEDRAL CHOIR v. ST. PETER PERAMBULATORS.—Played at Lower Close on August 20th, and resulted in a win for the Cathedral Choir by 25 runs. Corbyn, Smith I, and Hill played well for the Cathedral, and Lee and Alcock for the losing side. Cathedral Choir.—Hill 2, Smith I 4, England 0, Corbyn 11, Bewston 1, Self 9, Darken 8, Smith II 0, Day 3, Howard 0, Daynes 1, extras 13; total, 53. St. Peter Perambulators.—Lee 7, Alcock 1, Hubbard 2, Baldry 2, Betts 1 6, Parks 0, Bowers 0, Betts 2, Dix 1, Harvey 0, Page 0, extra 1; total, 12.

ST. PAUL'S v. BRYERS ROAD.—Played at Mousehold on August 17th, and resulted in a win for St. Paul's by 27 runs. The bowling of the Rev J Calvert and E M Jackson was good. The former captured four wickets for 6 runs, and the latter five for 9, and B E Jackson also batted well for the winners; while Lubbock bowled well for the losers. St Paul's—E M Jackson 5-1, Rev J Calvert 5-31, B E Jackson not out 19-11, G F Hill 3-10 not out, Gilbert Hill 0, H Hill 3-0 not out, Mann 0-9, Adcock 2, Breed 0, Ellis 1, Twiddy 0, extras 8-8; total 44-51. Bryers Road—Bayes 0, Browne 0, Ramabottom 3, Lubbock 5, Payne 2, Burton 2, Gibbs 0, High 1, Simpson 0, Kidden not out 3, extras 1; total 17.

THORPE VILLAGE v. NORWICH UNION.—Played at Thorpe on Thursday, and resulted in a win for the Village by 42 runs. Thorpe batted first, and thanks to Black (21) and Newstead (13), they were able to make 54 runs. On the N.U.F.C. going in to bat only Smith could make any stand against the bowling of Black and Newstead, who took five for 8 and four for 11 respectively. Thorpe Village—Pitts 1, Parker 5, Wright 8, Black 21, Newstead 13, Danborough 1, Gopp 0, Harper 4, Woodcock 2, Fox 5, Fenn not out 4, extras 3; total 54. Norwich Union—W. Wallace 2, W. C. Walton 3, Stone 0, Cross 0, Smith not out 11, Brown 1, Corbie 0, Coleman 2, Drane 0, Budd 0, Kershaw 0, extras 3; total 22.
